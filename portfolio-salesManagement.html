Sales Management System

The management of property sales, previously handled through external applications, often faced limitations in terms of customization and maintenance. To address this, an internal web-based application has been designed and integrated, covering unit management, SPPR document creation, KPR transaction management, and sales unit status reports, tracking the progress of sales through various stages, to better align with operational needs and facilitate easier management.

Technologies Used in This Project

  • Backend ASP.NET Core, C#
  • Frontend JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap
  • Database MySql

Project Impact

Organized Sales Data Management

Sales data management becomes more organized, reducing the potential for errors.

Faster Approval & Tracking

The approval process and sales status tracking are faster and more efficient.

Real-Time Performance Evaluation

The sales team can easily evaluate performance and track sales progress in real-time.

Accurate, Data-Driven Decisions

Enables more accurate, data-driven decision-making with reports that are automatically generated and up-to-date.
